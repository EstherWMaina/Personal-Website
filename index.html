About Me

Esther W. Maina is a Remote Sensing Expert leading projects on food systems and natural resources management. Her work involves developing innovative solutions for geospatial big data analytics and visualisation for decision support. She holds a Bachelor of Science from Dedan Kimathi University of Technology, specialising in Geospatial Information Science and a Master's in Space Entrepreneurship from Italy. She is a member of the International Society for Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing, a member of African Women in GIS, a graduate member of the Institute of Surveyors of Kenya, and AWS Cloud Practitioner certified.
